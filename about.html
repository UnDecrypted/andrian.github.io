About the Owner

My name is Andrian Pamungkas, and I am the owner of UnDecrypted.co. I have over 10 years of experience in the used motorcycle industry and am committed to providing high-quality used motorcycles to our customers. I believe that quality is key, and we always strive to offer the best options for our customers.

I started this business with the goal of providing a transparent and trustworthy used motorcycle buying experience. We only offer bikes that have gone through a thorough inspection process, so our customers can feel confident with every purchase made at our store.

Outside of work, I am an automotive enthusiast and always seek to learn about the latest motorcycle technologies and emerging market trends. I aim to continue helping customers find their dream bikes at fair prices and with exceptional service.
